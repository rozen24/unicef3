Understanding Adolescence

Adolescence is a critical period of growth and development. During this time, young people experience significant physical, emotional, and social changes. Understanding these changes helps in navigating this transformative phase with confidence and knowledge.

Key Changes During Adolescence

Boys' Physical Changes

Growth spurt in height and weight
Voice deepens and becomes hoarse
Increased muscle mass and strength
Development of facial hair

Girls' Physical Changes

Growth spurt and body development
Breast development begins
Menstrual cycle begins (menarche)
Hip widening and fat distribution

Mental & Emotional Changes

Enhanced thinking and reasoning
Mood swings and emotional sensitivity
Seeking independence and identity
Interest in peer relationships deepens
